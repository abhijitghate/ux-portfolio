    About me

    Hi again! My name is Anova S. Deve. I'm an interaction designer/ architect and have worked in maturing roles for developmental and humanitarian non-profits across various locations in Maharashtra and Kerala, India.

    Beyond work, I am a hobbyist programmer and occasionally spend my time singing to my ukulele and learning not to cook.

    Drop me an email to get in touch.
